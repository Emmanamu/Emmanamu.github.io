If you need to request time away from school, please complete a form (available from the office). We strongly encourage children to be in school whenever possible. Please also remember to sign in and out on the Signing In sheets everyday. As a Local Authority School, we are expected to monitor attendance and note on registers the reasons for absence. This attendance/absence data is requested by the Local Authority, the Department for Education and Ofsted. We must show how we improve children’s attendance. We now have a duty to contact you if you have not informed us already that your child is absent - for any reason. Please, therefore, do contact us as indicated above as it is very time consuming to ‘phone families if you have not contacted us. You can email through the website as well as emailing the office. www.purley.croydon.sch.uk Also, please make every effort to be here on time. Children will be marked as “late” if they arrive after 8.45 or 12.45pm. Special arrangements will be in place for those families whose hours need to be different and cannot avoid arriving later than 8.45. All After School children must be picked up by 6pm. If you have difficulty for any reason you must phone so that appropriate arrangements can be made. Please do not park on Zig Zag lines outside the school entrance. It is very dangerous for children and other motorists
